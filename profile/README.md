## Microsphere Projects

### Overview

With the continuous progress and popularization of Internet technology, the Internet industry has developed rapidly, and
the combination of online and offline business has gradually become a trend. The construction and iteration of the
Internet system improve the business ability and market competitiveness of enterprises.
As an important link in business support, the Microsphere platform is committed to realizing the modern Internet
distributed application framework, improving the advanced application technology of enterprises, and expanding the
professional vision and capabilities of the R&D team.

### Purpose and Scope

### Microservices

Different from the traditional monolithic application architecture, the microservice architecture adopted by the
platform modularizes and servitizes the functions provided by the platform, so that each of them can perform their own
duties and make full use of each other, greatly improve the efficiency of product research and development, and enable
users to experience the latest features of the platform at the first time. The fine-grained service makes the platform
better maintained, and each function is supported by independent domain experts, making the platform service stable and
reliable, while also reducing the cost of user use and Q&A.

### The Cloud Native

The platform runs on the world's leading cloud technology platform and is backed by the most advanced Cloud Native
technology facilities such as Kubernetes, Docker, Spring Cloud Alibaba, Apache Dubbo, etc., providing unmatched platform
stability and reducing customer deployment and operational costs. At the same time, both the technical team and business
services are leading the industry.

### Platform

The platform provides one-stop services, such as unified authentication, authorization, operation and maintenance
management, and other operations are completed on the platform.

### Big Data

Big data service is one of the core features of the platform, which provides a wealth of big data services such as
monitoring, data statistics, and analysis reports, and provides data support for R&D personnel to provide indicators,
customer business expansion, and business intelligence decision-making.

### High Availability

The platform is committed to providing 24x7 service capability with an availability of up to 99.999 %, which means that
the
service is not available for more than 5.26 minutes on a 365-day basis in a year. This is reduced to 26.3 seconds per
month. The high availability of services benefits from a distributed architecture that minimizes the risk of downtime.
At the same time, the platform is based on the architecture of microservices, which makes it possible to start
applications quickly.

### High Performance

The platform has been optimized by multiple dimensions such as system, configuration, and module to improve system
performance, improve resource utilization, standardize management and release processes, and avoid affecting the overall
performance.
